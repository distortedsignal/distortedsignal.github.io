An Old Faith, A New Friend

I thought it would be interesting to see what happens with someone put questions from various catechisms through ChatGPT.

I don't think this says anything interesting about God, or about the world around us. I think this says something interesting about culture, and how modern culture views faith and God.

Technical Considerations

In my experience, ChatGPT likes to answer questions with several paragraphs of text. Most catechism questions are meant to be answered tersely. Because of this, I modified the catechism questions to have the following formats:

In a sentence or two, {catechism question}

In the above example, the {catechism question} was replaced by the appropriate value for the appropriate question.
